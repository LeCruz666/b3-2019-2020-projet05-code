Soul to soul

Soul to Soul is the third studio album by Stevie Ray Vaughan and his group Double Trouble. It was released in 1985. Keyboardist Reese Wynans joined the group from this album.

In step

In Step is the fourth studio album by Stevie Ray Vaughan and his group Double Trouble. It was released in 1989 under the Epic label. It's a Texas Blues album.

Live alive

Live Alive is the first live album by Stevie Ray Vaughan and his group Double Trouble. It is a compilation from several live performances, recorded on July 16, 1985 at the Montreux Jazz Festival, on July 17 and 18, 1986 at the Austin Opera House and on July 19, 1986 at Dallas Starfest

Live at carnegie hall

Live at Carnegie Hall is the ninth album (and third live album) by American blues rock band Stevie Ray Vaughan and Double Trouble, released by Epic Records in July 1997. The album consists of live selections from their sold-out October 4, 1984 benefit concert at Carnegie Hall for the TJ Martell Foundation.
